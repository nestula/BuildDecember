Welcome to the Undecember Build Planner Community!

We're thrilled to have you join us on this journey to create the ultimate Undecember Build Planner! Whether you're a seasoned veteran optimizing your endgame build or a new player looking for guidance, this tool is designed to help you plan, refine, and perfect your builds with ease.

Our Mission

Our goal is simple: to build the most comprehensive, user-friendly, and feature-rich build planner for Undecember. We are continuously working to enhance the planner, adding new tools, refining calculations, and implementing community-driven improvements.

Community-Driven Development

This project is powered by you, the players! We believe that the best tools are built with the community in mind, which is why we value your feedback, feature suggestions, and bug reports. Every suggestion helps us fine-tune the planner to better serve your needs.

Your input directly shapes the future of this tool, and we're committed to listening, iterating, and evolving based on the needs of our users. If you encounter a bug, have an idea for a new feature, or just want to share your thoughts, we encourage you to reach out! Every piece of feedback is invaluable to us.

Stay Updated

We will be regularly posting updates, patch notes, and feature previews to keep you informed about what's new and what's coming next. Our team is always hard at work refining the planner, ensuring that it remains a cutting-edge tool for all players.

Thank You for Being Here!

Your support and enthusiasm fuel our passion for making this planner the best it can be. We appreciate every member of this community and are excited to grow, innovate, and optimize together.
